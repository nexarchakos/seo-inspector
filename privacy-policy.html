Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

SEO Inspector does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app performs SEO analysis locally on your device and does not transmit any data to remote servers.

Advertising and Tracking

SEO Inspector uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA), IP address, device type, operating system version, and usage data in order to serve relevant ads.

In accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, you will be asked for permission before any tracking occurs. If you decline, the app will still function and non-personalized ads may be shown instead.

Learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Third-Party SDKs

The app includes third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob, which may collect anonymized data for performance and advertising purposes. These services operate under their own privacy policies.
